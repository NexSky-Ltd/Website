How we charge

Simplicity and alignment are not just principles — they extend to how we are compensated.
Two components.
Complete transparency.
Our fee structure is built on two simple components — no product commissions, no retrocessions, no hidden charges. You always know exactly what you pay and why.
To discuss specific fee levels tailored to your situation, please reach out directly.
Monthly Membership Fee
A fixed monthly fee gives you full access to the NexSky platform — covering all four pillars of service: investing, lifestyle, legacy, and administration. One invoice, no surprises.
Management Fee
A management fee charged on assets under oversight — aligning our incentives directly with the performance and growth of your wealth.
No Retrocessions. Ever.
We do not receive commissions from product providers. Our only revenue comes from you — ensuring permanent alignment of interests.
